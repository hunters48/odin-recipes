Hamburger Recipe

A classic, juicy Hamburger Recipe made with ground chuck, a simple burger seasoning, and all of the classic burger toppings. Plus, tips for preparing the meat and cooking the burgers to they are tender and juicy!

Ingredients

  • Pickles
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Slice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cheese
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Shape Beef Into Patty
  2. Add Salt and Pepper to Meat
  3. Cook Burger
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Toast Bun
  6. Place Burger on Bun
  7. Add Pickles and Tomato

Super Easy Pasta Recipe

Fast, easy, ready in 15 minutes, and my family loves it — that’s my idea of the perfect weeknight dinner. This easy spaghetti recipe checks off all those boxes and it’s comforting, hearty, and great for chilly winter nights.

It’s as easy as browning ground beef, adding your favorite pasta sauce, and tossing with spaghetti. But here’s where the time-saving ingredient comes into play.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Brown Ground Beef
  3. Add Pasta Sauce To Meat
  4. Mix With Pasta
  5. Plate. Add Basil and Parmesan Cheese
